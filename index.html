This is a complete end-to-end algorithmic trading system (not just an analytical engine) that implements the "Buy No Early" strategy to exploit retail trader bias in binary prediction markets.

ğŸ¯ Core Strategy

Historically, only 22% of sensational binary markets resolve as "Yes". Retail traders systematically overprice "Yes" outcomes in the first few minutes after market launch due to wishful thinking and hype. This system:

  1. Detects new markets within 30 minutes of launch
  2. Analyzes pricing inefficiencies using Bayesian probability estimation
  3. Executes "Buy No" orders when edge > 10%
  4. Manages positions with stop-loss, take-profit, and time-based exits
  5. Closes positions as hype fades and prices normalize

  • Position Sizing: Kelly Criterion with 25% safety factor
